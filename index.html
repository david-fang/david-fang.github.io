A little something about myself

Hi there! I'm David, a software engineer by day and a designer by night. Now don't get me wrong. I'm not some vigilante who goes around trying to redesign the city in the shadows. Although the thought of being Batman may have crossed my mind during my childhood years, I'm actually just more creative after a filling dinner. Outside of my professional hobbies, I make time to practice jazz piano, replicate my mom's green bean stirfry, doze off to LoFi HipHop, and keep my plants alive.

Some of the things I've worked on
“A designer knows he has achieved perfection not when there is nothing left to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.”
- Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

We live in a world that is by our design. Everything that surrounds and envelops us is arranged, either by us or by nature. As a designer and developer, good design to me means more than just creating something pleasing to the eye. It means finding a form of self-expression that can leave an impression beyond words. It means creating tools that will allow others to shape their lives and the communities around them. And most importantly, it means embarking on a journey where innovation, not perfection, is rewarded.
